h4. *Position*

First you should select what [[Positions | Position]] you would like to play since your decisions here may influence what options you have for race/family, this is your role in combat. Teams usually have at least one member of each position.


h4. *Background*

Next you will need to choose a [[Races | Race]]. If you choose to be human (with some exceptions see [[Lo Po Bia Family | Lo Po Bia Family]]) you can additionally choose to be part of one of the [[Ten Great Families | Ten Great Families]]. This will affect your stats and potentially what kind of skills you can learn. 

Additionally, you could choose to be an [[Irregular | Irregular]], this will have a significant effect on the story and you may become the narrative centerpiece of the story.
