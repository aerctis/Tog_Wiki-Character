
Both the leveling system and stats are work quite differently to DnD.

h4. *Leveling System*

The leveling system has some significant changes:
* No fixed level cap--your level will be ~10x higher
* Much faster leveling rate 
* Lower power gain per level (often only base [[Stats | stat increases]])

h4. *Level Up Rewards*

You don't get to see exactly what rewards you get on level up, but you can look at what levels are required for certain skills on your [[Position Skills | position specifc skill page.]] 

You can also see the stats increases and stat points you get per level on your [[Position Stats | position specifc stat page.]] 

You will occasionally get skill slots and frequently get skill points. For more info see [[Skills | Skills]]

 You will also need to <a href="https://tog.obsidianportal.com/wikis/stats#character_health">recalculate your HP upon leveling up.</a>
