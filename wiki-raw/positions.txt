[[Fisherman | Fisherman]]
The main offence of the battle, Fishermen are to dash into the heart of the enemy force and decimate their forces, usually through physical attacks.

[[Wave Controller | Wave Controller]]
Wave Controllers are the directors of the battle, aiding their comrades by healing their wounds, annihilating enemy forces or subtly changing the tide of the battle, all through the use of [[Shinsu | Shinsu]]. There are many variations of this Position.

[[Spear Bearer | Spear Bearer]]
Spear Bearers act like snipers for the team, throwing their spears with deadly accuracy, but they can also serve a purpose similar to the Fisherman's by using their spear like a staff for close-combat. Technically speaking, they take all long-range matters into their own hands.

[[Light Bearer | Light Bearer]]
Light Bearers are responsible for gathering, storing and delivering crucial knowledge to their team-mates while guiding them with the light of their Lighthouse. Light Bearers are usually the most support-oriented position.

[[Anima | Anima]]
An Anima controls living beings, commonly [[Shinheuh | Shinheuh]]. It seems it's more a talent than a technique, as one cannot just become an Anima through learning.

[[Guide | Guide]]
The Guide is considered a Special Position, but it plays no specific role in combat. Guides lead the way for the team, usually with their ability to see into the future and excellent analysis skills. It is a very rare Position and they are considered "blessed" by The Tower.
